LENSCRAFT MEDIA INSTITUTE

LensCraft Media Institute is a creative training hub based in Joska, Kenya, specializing in videography, video editing, photography, and digital content creation. The institution was established with the goal of empowering young creatives with practical media skills needed in today’s fast-growing digital world.

At LensCraft, students are trained through hands-on learning using modern equipment and real-world production projects. The institute focuses on storytelling, camera operation, lighting techniques, sound production, editing software mastery, and content strategy for social media platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.

Beyond technical skills, LensCraft Media Institute also emphasizes creativity, branding, and entrepreneurship, helping learners turn their passion into income-generating careers. The school works closely with local media houses, content creators, and production companies to provide internship opportunities and industry exposure.

Located in Joska, along Kangundo Road, the institute serves students from Nairobi and surrounding counties, offering both short courses and intensive certification programs. With a mission to nurture the next generation of African storytellers, LensCraft continues to grow as a trusted name in media education.

Core services: Videography training, video editing classes, content creation coaching, photography workshops, and digital media mentorship programs.
